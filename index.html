Entel Roaming

Manage the invoice between telecommunications companies around the world.

Telecommunications companies around the world share their network connections through roaming, so we, the consumers, can still have comunications with our beloved ones. In this scenario the external company will rent the infrastructure to the local company to keep the service up for the consumers. This also works the other way around. This process of "renting" the antenas to other telecommunications companies needs to be managed at large scales so the companies knows exactly how much do they need to pay (or receive) by the end of the month. This project manages all the incoming and outcoming invoices.

  • Date: July 2014
  • Client: Entel
  • Category: Software development
